where touching the ground with anything other than a foot loses the match.

Bökh means "firmness, reliability, vitality, wrestler", from Mongolic root *bekü

Үндэсний бөх

"firm, hard, solid; fighter, strong man"

Үндэсний бөх

possibly from Turkic *böke "warrior" < "big snake".

Үндэсний бөх

Wrestling is the most important of the Mongolian culture's historic "Three Manly Skills",

Үндэсний бөх

that also include horsemanship and archery.

Genghis Khan considered wrestling to be an important way to keep his army in good physical shape and combat ready.
